📥 Bulk Import from Excel

Rules:

Copy from Excel and paste here. Two formats are supported:

Format 1 (Bg/Cn): Count | Lot | Brand | Bg:BagWt; Cn:ConeWt

30/1 LOT-001 GMS Bg:50; Cn:2.08

Bg:50 = 50kg bag, Cn:2.08 = cone weight → 50÷2.08 = 24 Cone

Format 2 (Direct): Count | Lot | Brand | ConeQty | NetWeight

30/1 LOT-001 GMS 24 2.40
